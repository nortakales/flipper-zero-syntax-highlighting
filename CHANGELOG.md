# Change Log

## [1.2.0] - 2024-05-05

- Added support for:
  - `.ibtn`
  - `.u2f`
  - `.fmf` (Flipper Music File)
- Fixed `NECext`, `NEC42` and `NEC42ext` highlighting in `.ir` and `.xr`

## [1.1.0] - 2024-04-18

- Added support for:
  - `.picopass` (Picopass app)
  - `.xr` (Cross Remote app)

## [1.0.0] - 2024-04-06

- Initial release
- Includes basic syntax highlighting for:
  - `.ir`
  - `.sub`
  - `.nfc`
  - `.rfid`
